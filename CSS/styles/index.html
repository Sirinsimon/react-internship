Football players

Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players in the history of the sport. Known for his exceptional dribbling, precise passing, and prolific goal-scoring, he plays as a forward or midfielder for Major League Soccer club Inter Miami and captains the Argentina national team.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo (born February 5, 1985) is a Portuguese professional footballer broadly considered one of the greatest players in the history of the sport. He is a five-time Ballon d'Or winner and, in 2024, became the first men's player to officially score 900 career goals..

Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior

Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior (born February 5, 1992) is a Brazilian professional footballer widely regarded as one of the most gifted and technically skilled players of his generation. He captains Brazilian club Santos and the Brazil national team, having cemented his status as a global icon and Brazil's all-time leading international goalscore.

Kylian Mbappé

Kylian Mbappé Lottin (born December 20, 1998) is a French professional footballer widely regarded as one of the best players in the world. He plays as a forward for Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain and the France national team. Known for his exceptional speed, dribbling skills, and goal-scoring ability, Mbappé has achieved significant success at a young age, including winning the FIFA World Cup with France in 2018.

Mohamed Salah

Mohamed Salah Hamed Mahrous Ghaly (born June 15, 1992) is an Egyptian professional footballer widely regarded as one of the best players in the world. He plays as a forward for Premier League club Liverpool and the Egypt national team. Known for his exceptional speed, dribbling skills, and goal-scoring ability, Salah has achieved significant success at both the club and international levels, including winning the Premier League Golden Boot and being named the African Footballer of the Year multiple times.
