Tea Preparation Guide

Traditional Masala Chai

Ingredients

  • • Fresh water
  • • tea leaves or tea bags
  • • Sugar or honey
  • • Milk

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. 1
    Boil the Water

    Heat fresh water until it reaches a rolling boil for optimal extraction

  2. 2
    Add Tea Leaves

    Place your chosen tea leaves or bags into a pre-warmed teapot

  3. 3
    Pour

    Pour the boiling water over the tea and let it steep for 3-5 minutes

  4. 4
    Serve

    Pour the perfectly brewed tea into your favorite cup

  5. 5
    Enjoy

    Add milk and sugar if desired, then enjoy your perfect cup of tea
